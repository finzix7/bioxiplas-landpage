## Product Description [.text-h4]

Bag made from a blend of naturally biodegradable polymers, ideal for disposal in home or industrial composting systems. Complies with EN-13432:2000 and Directive 94/62/EC.

### Key Features [.text-h5]

- Compostable and biodegradable  
- Rapid degradation (2–12 months)  
- High strength and elasticity  
- European certifications (OK Compost Industrial)  
- Color: Opaque natural white
